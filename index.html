Mission Statement

BlueMatrix Technologies is an agricultural technology startup specializing in crop disease detection powered by machine learning. We'll help farmers surgically manage crop health with detailed field analytics.

Our Projects

Disease Detection in Corn

We have successfully produced a computer vision model identifying the southern blight disease in sweet corn crops. We achieved this with a relatively small dataset of 1000 images. Our next step is to develop a model from a significantly larger dataset to increase accuracy and make it generalizable to more diseases and all stages of corn growth.
